Manage research and IP from proposal to commercialization

Academic Research & Grants Information System
ARGIS is a web-based enterprise system which provides users direct access to the information needed for managing funded research. The system tracks proposals, funding, licenses, and other agreements. The system also allows users to report and document hazards and conflicts of interest that impact compliance requirements.

Analyze research financials and located assets

Personal Access to Research Information System
PARIS is an enterprise web system that allows researchers, chairs, deans and administrators to view information regarding their research proposals, awards, budget and expenditures, deliverables and documents. The system also provides information about research equipment valued at $10K or above at UCF.

Run research reports for UCF

A University Resources Online Reporting Appilcation
AURORA is an enterprise web system which provides on-demand or subscription-based reports on funded research activity at UCF. Users can view reports on expenditures, new funding, and current burn rates among other reports, many of which are available at both aggregate and detailed levels.

Internal Opportunities
The Internal Opportunities site includes information about Limited Submission opportunities from federal, state, and private programs, and UCF Internal Programs (seed funds, matched funds) facilitated through the Office of Research & Commercialization (ORC).

Disclose and review potential conflicts of interest

Conflict of Interest & Commitment System
The Conflict of Interest & Commitment System provides information regarding UCF's policies and allows the submission and review of disclosure statements relating to conflict of interest and commitment on research activity

Manage research and IP from proposal to commercialization

My Online reView of E-Docs
MOVE is a web system for submission of research or other contracts for legal review by Office of General Counsel. Contracts can also be sent to ad-hoc reviewers before review by OGC. The system provides a template library with pre-approved clauses/templates for contracts.

ORC repository for research-related documents

The Electronic Research Archive
TERA is a web system provides a digital repository for electronic documents that pertain to sponsored research projects or Research Foundation. The TERA documents for a research project can be accessed by the researchers and research administrators in PARIS.

Effort reporting & certification

Effort Reporting and Certification
This enterprise web system facilitates UCF's effort reporting and certification process in compliance with UCF's effort reporting policies, federal policy OMB Circular A-21 section J.10., NIH Salary Limitation on Grants, and other policies that govern the need to provide certification of an individual's sponsored or non-sponsored effort.

Track data for Business Incubator Program sites and clients

Business Incubation Program Reporting
This web-system tracks site-level and company-level data for monthly, quarterly and annual BIP reporting. Information tracked includes site affiliation, client type and status, DUNS and EIN number, entry and exit date, CEO/founder contact information, headcount, grants awarded, angel investment, revenue, award funding, milestones, and notes.

Institutional Review Board submission and approval (Not Single Sign-On)

Integrated Research Information Systems
The iRIS enterprise system facilitates Institution Review Board management with streamlined workflows for managing the submission, monitoring, renewal, and termination of research involving human subjects.

Collaborative Institutional Training Initiative

Collaborative Institutional Training Initiative
The CITI Program at the University of Miami provides web-based training materials to serve millions of learners at academic institutions, government agencies, and commercial organizations in the U.S. and around the world. CITI provides educational training content that promotes the quality of and public trust in the research enterprise.

Manage access to proposal development training material

Secure Docs
Secure Docs is a web system that lets UCF faculty and researchers access protected content and training materials related to proposal development using their NID (Single Sign-On).

eFacilities and Administrative Cost System

eFacilities and Administrative Cost System
Huron's efacs® system provides a flexible web-based cost system for the calculation and negotiation of the facilities and administrative (F&A) rates. The data models available and created within efacs are used by UCF sponsored research to determine the appropriate F&A rate to be applied on all university research facilities.

Manage and review contracts submitted to Office of General Counsel

CobbleStone Systems
CobbleStone is a cloud software solution that allows any university employee to submit a contract that requires legal review from one of the four legal offices. The system allows the legal office to gather internal approvals and secure signatures to execute agreements and allows the submitter to monitor their contract's progression during the review lifecycle.
